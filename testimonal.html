The only way to fix this problem is at its roots. This will encourage others to have the courage to report their bullying Keep copies of all texts, emails or any other communications from the cyberbully in case there is an investigation Be careful who you give your mobile number or email address to and tell your friends not to pass on your details without checking first Be careful who you accept as 'friends' on social media sites
